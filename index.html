  • Hydration is the key to good results; drink 100 ounces of water every day for three consecutive days prior to your appointment.
  • Enjoy a long soak in an epsom salt bath the evening before your appointment.
  • Gently exfoliate areas that you wish to have sugar applied to two days prior.
  • Refrain from applying deodorant, body oils, and lotions the day of your appointment.
  • Arrive in comfortable, loose fitting clothing.
  • Avoid hot baths, steam rooms, saunas, or heavy sweating for 24-48 hours post-treatment.
  • Refrain from applying oils, deodorants, or heavily fragranced products to treated areas for the first 24 hours.
  • Keep the area clean and moisturized with a gentle, fragrance-free lotion.
  • Avoid direct sun exposure or tanning beds for at least 48 hours and apply sunscreen to protect the skin.
  • Wear loose, breathable clothing to reduce irritation in treated areas.
  • Gently exfoliate treated areas 2-3 times per week to help prevent ingrown hairs.
